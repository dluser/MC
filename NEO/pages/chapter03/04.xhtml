Program Guide

Medical

High Option (In-Network)

  • $300 deductible per person per year, $900 max for family
  • $2,800 out of pocket maximum per person, $8,400 max for family
  • Office visits: $20 (deductible waived)
  • Specialty visits: $30 (deductible waived)
  • $0 co-pay for preventative services
  • Plan pays 80% and you pay 20% for allowable expenses once annual deductible is met

Low Option (In-Network)

  • $1,500 deductible per person per year, $4,500 max for family
  • $3,500 out of pocket maximum per person, $8,750 max for family
  • Office visits: $25 (deductible waived)
  • Specialty visits:$25 (deductible waived)
  • $0 co-pay for preventative services
  • Plan pays 75% and you pay 25% for allowable expenses, once annual deductible is met

Prescriptions

Local Pharmacy
(excluding Walgreens)
  • 30 day supply
  • $5 copay (generic)
  • 30% copay (preferred)
  • Between $18 -$ 50, 70% copay (non-preferred)
Walgreens Pharmacy
  • 30 day supply
  • $10 copay (generic)
  • 30% copay (preferred)
  • Between $23 - $55, 70% copay (non-preferred)
Mail Order Pharmacy
  • 90 day supply
  • $12.50 copay (generic)
  • $45 copay (preferred), 70% copay (non-preferred)

Dental

Preventative Services Plan

2 Oral exams per year
2 Cleanings per year
Basic Services Plan pays 80%
$50/$150 Deductible per person/family
Plan Year Jan 1st - Dec 31st

High Option (In-Network)

  • Major Services Plan Pays 50%
  • Orthodontic Plan Pays 50%
  • Calendar year max pay per person $1500

Low Option (In-Network)

  • No coverage for Major or Orthodontic Services
  • Calendar year max pay per person $1250

Vision

Eye Exam
  • One per year
  • $10 co-pay (In-Network)
Basic Frames
  • One pair of frames every two years
    • If frame selection is not included in the "Tower Collection", the plan will apply a $40 credit towards the purchase of the selected frames
    • Additional options are available at discounted fixed fees
Contacts
  • Once every year (in lieu of glasses)
  • No co-pay for standard lenses or a $110 credit plus 15% discount towards the purchase of other types of lenses

*Two year commitment required

Life Insurance

Basic Term Life

CNM pays 100% of premium
Automatic Enrollment for eligible employees

Coverage
  • $50,000 Term Life Insurance
  • $50,000 additional payment for AD&D (Accidental Death/Dismemberment)

Additional & Dependent Life

Employee pays 100% of premium

Employee Coverage
  • From one to three times your annual salary
  • Maximum coverage of $600,000
  • Accidental death & dismemberment coverage is included for employee only
Spouse/Domestic Partner Coverage
  • One times your annual salary or 50% of your additional life election amount (whichever is less)
Child Coverage (up to age 26)
  • $5,000

Long-Term Disability

  • CNM pays 80% of the premium
  • 30 day waiting period for benefit to begin
  • Plan pays 66.66% of the insured pre-disability earnings (up to a maximum of $5,000 per month)
  • Premiums for LTD are waived while receiving benefit
  • Pre-existing condition rules do apply
