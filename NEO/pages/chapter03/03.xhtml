Who is Eligible?

  • Spouse, Natural Children, Stepchildren, Adopted Children, (up to age 26)
  • Domestic Partner (Affidavit required)
  • Domestic Partner Children (Affidavit required)

Part-Time Faculty

Offered benefits term-by-term based on contact hours.

Contact hours are tracked during a 12 month "measurement period" to determine eligibility for a 12 month "stability period" the following year.

Stability is earned by averaging 12 or more contact hours during a measurement period.

Employees must enroll in benefits during open enrollment to be eligible for the stability period. Failure to enroll during open enrollment will be interpreted as a denial of coverage for reporting purposes

The entire employee share of monthly insurance premiums for a term in which a part-time instructor has no class assignments must be prepaid prior to the start of the term. Failure to prepay may result in a loss of coverage.

Employee Responsibility

Supportive Documentation Required for all Dependents

  • Social Security number for each dependent enrolled
  • Spouse Enrollment/Marriage (Marriage certificate)
  • Partner Enrollment (Domestic Partner Affidavit)

Qualifying Event Supportive Documenation

  • Divorce (Final divorce decree)
  • Child Enrollment/Birth of Child (Birth certificate)
  • Adoption of child (Formal adoption paperwork)
  • Involuntary loss of coverage (Involuntary loss of coverage letter and marriage certificate, domestic partner affidavit, birth certificates, etc. if applicable)

*Provide all necessary documentation timely

First-Time Enrollment & Changes

New-Hire Enrollment

  • 31 days to enroll from hire date
  • Benefits effective 1st of the month
  • 1 month premium due in advance

Changes to Enrollment

  • 31 days from qualifying event (look above for applicable events)
  • Examples: Birth, Marriage, Promotion, Divorce, etc.

1095-C Tax Form

All full-time employees, as well as any employee who was enrolled in healthcare coverage through CNM at any time in 2015, will receive a Form 1095-C from CNM.

Contains information about your healthcare coverage that will be required when completing your tax return.

Watch for an email in December to remind you to log on to MyCNM and give consent to receive your 1095 electronically.
