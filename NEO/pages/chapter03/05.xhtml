Tuition Waiver

Employee Tuition Waiver
  • Regular full-time are eligible for up to 9-18 credit hours per year
  • Regular part-time are eligible for up to 4-9 credit hours per year
Dependent Tuition Waiver
  • Spouses, domestic partners, and dependent children until age 24 are eligible based on employee position
  • Regular full-time are eligible for up to 9-18 credit hours per term
  • Regular part-time are eligible for up to 4-9 credit hours per term

*Applies to tuition, registration fees and technology fees
**With supervisor approval, Full-Time employees may be granted 3 hours of release time
***Tax Implications may apply please talk with a payroll representative for more details

Educational Benefit
Tuition Reimbursement

Full-Time employee benefit
  • Receive tuition reimbursement for institutions other than CNM
  • Receive up to 18 credit hours per year
  • Does not cover any non-tuition related expenses

Tuition costs are reimbursed at the actual cost or at the current University of New Mexico tuition rate, whichever is less.

This reimbursement does not cover late fees, lab fees, registration fees, books/supplies, or any other non-tuition related fees. Courses must be taken on the employees own time.

Important

**Part-time employees are not eligible for tuition reimbursement
