Quick Definitions

  • Full-Time: 40 hrs/week
  • Part-Time: < 40 hrs/week
  • Limited-Term: employed for specific terms
  • Temporary: employment does not exceed a year
  • Casual: <10 hrs/weekÂ 
  • Student Employee: Part-time no benefits

Non-Exempt/Exempt


Non-exempt Employee- An hourly employee, is paid for all overtime hours worked.

  • Regular- 6 months
  • Instructional Support Staff- 12 Months
  • Casual (on call)- No trial period
  • Limited term and grant funded- No trial period
  • Temporary staff- No trial period
  • Student Employees- No trial period


Exempt Employee- A salaried employee, not paid for overtime hours.

  • Regular-28 Months
  • Full-Time Faculty- 7 terms
  • Part-Time Faculty- No trial period, term by term
  • Limited term and grant funded positions- No trial period
  • Temporary staff- No trial period
