Leave

CNM recognizes the value of its employees by providing various leave options based upon the employee's job classification, the Employee Handbook, or the respective Collective Bargaining Agreement. 

Regular, non-instructional employees accrue annual leave based upon their years of service with the College as shown in this table. Eligible part-time employees earn annual leave based upon their years of service with the college accrued on a prorated basis based on a forty hour work week. Senior Administrative Staff on annual contracts accrue annual leave in accordance with the terms of their respective contracts.

Employees accrue annual leave each pay period.  If an employee does not work a full pay period, the employee's annual leave is prorated for the hours worked.  Annual leave is earned during all paid leaves authorized by the College.

Important

  • An employee's unused annual leave balance may not exceed 30 days or 240 hours.
  • Advanced approval of annual leave is required.

Employees must request and receive approval of annual leave in advance from their immediate supervisors.  Failure to request and receive approval in advance may be grounds for denial of the leave and/or docking of pay.

Sick Leave

Sick leave can be used for sick-related time away from work including doctor's appointments.

Annual Leave

Eligible employees may earn, accrue, use, and are paid for annual leave.

Personal Leave

Paid time off given to employees for the purpose of attending to personal business.

Bereavement Leave

Leave for each death in the immediate family or anyone who lives with the employee.

Medical Leave

All employees who have been employed with CNM for one (1) or more years and have worked a minimum of 1,250 hours within the past year are eligible for up to 480 hours of unpaid medical leave in a rolling 12-month period.

Holiday Leave

All regular, full-time employees receive a minimum of sixteen (16) days of paid holiday leave.

Catastrophic Leave

In the unfortunate event of an employee's unexpected severe personal illness where all of the employee's personal leave banks have been depleted, CNM offers the ability for other employees to donate annual or personal leave to help the employee through their time of need.

Court Leave

Full pay is granted to all regular full and part-time employees for court legal processes, including jury duty, subpoena response, or other legal processes which require an absence from duty other than personal matters.

Military Leave

An employee is granted military leave in accordance with all state and federal laws. The first fifteen (15) work days of such leave is paid for employees in the National Guard or Reserve.

Professional Development Leave

Professional leave with pay is available for participation in College-related professional activities upon the approval of the designated supervisor.
